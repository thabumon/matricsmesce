A B O U T

We are the active student association MATRICS
under the Department of Computer Science & Engineering

which is intended for the development of skills in technical areas by motivating their research and innovation attitudes.
It organises several events for the holistic growth of the students and staff, while promoting technological advancements.




Vision

To empower the students to play a vital and leading role as technologist in the rapidly changing arena of Computer Science and Engineering and also prepare them to contribute to the advancement of society and mankind.

Mission

Impart high quality technical education to help the students to respond to the global happenings and take up research work to create knowledge in the field, through rigorous and comprehensive teaching learning process and inculcate in them the ethical and moral values for the society.

Goal

To match the proper hardware and software to meet the technological, scientific, and administrative needs of businesses and industries.
